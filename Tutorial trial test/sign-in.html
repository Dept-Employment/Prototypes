Let's get started

Go to myGov and sign in to your jobactive account to get started in the trial.

OR

If you don't already have a job seeker account here on the jobactive website, you need to set one up.

  1. Go to myGov.
  2. Add Australian JobSearch to your myGov services.
  3. Follow the steps to link your jobactive job seeker account.
