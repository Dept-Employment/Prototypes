Find a Provider

What a provider can do for your business

Financial help to hire new staff

Your business can get up to $10,000 (GST inclusive) when you hire a new staff through a provider

Find out more about wage subsidies

Professional recruitment services at no cost to you

Providers work with you to find the best candidate for your business. They can:

  • Screen and recommend candidates based on your job description.
  • Arrange for a job seeker to do training specific to the needs of your business.
  • offer continued support as your new employee settles in to your business.
Get help with your recruitment

Trial before you hire

Reduce you rrisk and make sure you get the right staff. Host an internship through PaTH or trial a new employee through the National Work Experience Programme before you hire them.
